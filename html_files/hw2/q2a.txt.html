1    The exploit we take advantage of in this section is the short length of the message. 
2    In our case, the credit card PIN code is sent in a separated message by it self. 
3    Thus, we know that the message length is 4 charactes long, and there are 10^4 = 10,000 possible PIN codes. 
4    The method just exhaust over all possible PIN codes, encrypting each one of them, and comparing 
5     the results with our encrypted text. 
6    One of the PIN codes must match because the encryption algorithm is deterministic.
