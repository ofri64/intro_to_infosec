1    - This firewall design is susceptible to a DoS attack 
2     
3    - The vulnerability is the fact that the firewall 
4    allocates memory resources for every SYN packet received by it. 
5     
6    - If all we wish to do is perform a Dos attack we can simply 
7    generate a sequence of SYN requests, until we get blocked, each time from a different ip. 
8     
9    - It doesn't have to be our ip, or an ip address of a host we control because 
10    we are not interested in getting back any response from the attacked host. 
11    
12   - All we want to do is to overflow the attacked host memory consumption 
13   (and thus deny it from responding to legitimate requests)
